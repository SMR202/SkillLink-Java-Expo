# SkillLink — Product Requirements Document

## App Overview
SkillLink is a two-sided mobile service marketplace where Clients post jobs and book skilled Providers. The app aims for a premium, clean, modern, and trustworthy feel, similar to a polished fintech or professional services platform.

## Design Direction
*   **Style:** Clean white base, bold typography, generous whitespace.
*   **Primary Color:** #4F46E5 (Deep Indigo/Violet)
*   **Background:** #FFFFFF with #F9FAFB surface cards.
*   **Text:** #111827 (Primary), #6B7280 (Muted).
*   **Accents:** Success (#10B981), Warning (#F59E0B), Error (#EF4444).
*   **Cards:** 16px rounded corners, soft shadow, generous padding.
*   **Buttons:** 12px radius, 48px height. Primary (filled indigo), Secondary (outlined).
*   **Status Pills:** Color-coded for Pending, Accepted, Declined, Completed, and Open.
*   **Typography:** Bold headings, clean body text, clear hierarchy.

## User Roles & Navigation
*   **Client:** Home (Browse/Jobs), My Jobs, Bookings, Chat, Profile.
*   **Provider:** Dashboard, Jobs, Bookings, Earnings, Chat, Profile.
*   **Admin:** Dashboard, Users, Reviews, Analytics.

## Key Screens (Summary)
1. **Auth:** Onboarding, Sign Up (Role Toggle), Log In, Forgot Password.
2. **Client Flow:** Home (Provider Discovery & Job Board), Post a Job, Proposals, Provider Profile, Booking Flow, Checkout, Reviews.
3. **Provider Flow:** Dashboard (Stats/Earnings), Job Board (Find Jobs), Proposal Submission, Booking Management, Earnings & Analytics.
4. **Shared:** Chat List & Room, Profile & Settings.
5. **Admin:** Dashboard, User Management, Review Moderation, Analytics.
